Thank You for Signing Up!

We appreciate your decision to sign up with TransfusioTrack. Welcome aboard!

  • We have received all your details. Now, sit back and relax. Our executive will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your blood bank credentials and details, including name, address, phone number, and most importantly, registration and other documents.
  • Once verified, you will become our trusted partner. This verification is part of the onboarding process, ensuring the security and safety of public health. It also guarantees that only authenticated banks with proper government approval and compliance onboard TransfusioTrack, ensuring complete transparency and trust for the users, especially the patients.

Please note that if the documents provided are not in compliance with government regulations, we may not be able to proceed with the onboarding process. Our commitment is to maintain the highest standards and provide a trustworthy platform for our users.

Upon successful verification, you will gain access to your credentials through TransfusioTrack.
We will also provide a personalized, user-friendly dashboard to tailore your needs.
